Ever since you opened your eyes and found out that the place you are living on , the place you call home is The Earth .The Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet that supports life. It is covered by land and water, with about 71% of its surface made up of oceans. The Earth provides air, water, and natural resources that living things need to survive. It rotates on its axis, causing day and night, and revolves around the Sun, creating the seasons. Protecting the Earth is important for the well-being of all living organisms.
